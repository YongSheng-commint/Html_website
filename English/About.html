About Veltrix Motors

Veltrix Motors began in 1998 when James Veltrix, a passionate engineer, dreamed of building smarter, more efficient cars. Starting in a small garage, he created the Veltrix One, a revolutionary hybrid sedan that caught the industry's attention.

By 2010, Veltrix launched its first electric vehicle, the Nova, setting new standards for range and performance. Despite challenges, the company pushed forward, introducing the Hyperion in 2015, a luxury sports car powered by solar energy and hydrogen fuel cells.

Today, Veltrix Motors leads in electric and AI-driven vehicles, committed to innovation and sustainability, shaping the future of mobility.
